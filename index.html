Stage: 0 Attempts: 0 Max. Attmepts: 10

How to Play

  • Player have 10 attempts for each stage.
  • The pikachu walls will be disappeard once the Poke ball hit Pikachu

    • 1. Press Enter to Start Game.

    2. Press left and/or right arrow keys to move trainer Ash left and/or right.

    3. Press up or down arrow keys to control the trajectory of the Poke Ball.

    4. Press and hold Space bar to control the power of Poke Ball.

    5. Catch a Charmander to move onto next stage, not Pikachu.
